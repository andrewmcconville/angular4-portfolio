UX Philosophy

I believe a great user experience is when the learning curve is kept low, which I'm always trying to iterate toward through design thinking methodologies.

This requires an understanding of both user and business goals. Great UX also thrives on design consistency, which takes the form of a well documented and maintained design system. Teach users your design language once by providing them consistent interaction patterns and a consistent look and feel.
