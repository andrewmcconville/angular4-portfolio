Hi, I’m Andrew McConville; I build, document, and perform usability tests on hi-fi prototypes and production components. I believe iterating quickly and testing often keep a product in alignment with user research and business goals while delivering a focused, consistent, experience.

Work Examples

Stack Overflow User Research

I started conducting user research with Stack Overflow in 2017 for my Graduate Certificate in User Experience at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. The project involved feedback and mentorship from Stack Overflow’s product and user research team.

Mesmer

Mesmer aims to help students visualize their degree progress by organizing courses within semesters and allowing students the ability to quickly rearrange courses, add hypothetical grades and graph credit count.

DK Handbook

Combining text along side visuals in easy to read two page spreads the print edition of the DK Handbook is a college level English 102 textbook by Anne Frances Wysocki and‎ Dennis Lynch. Producing a digital version for tablets allowed for the use of animations, audio, video, and mini-games at a high level of interactivity.
