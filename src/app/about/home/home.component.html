Hi, I’m Andrew McConville.
I'm a UX professional with a background in B2B SaaS applications. I enjoy playing an integral role in cross functional teams that are dedicated to user-centered design.

UX Research & Design Philosophy

I’ve always been curious why an experience can delight one person yet bring anxiety and frustration to another. I believe discovering user needs and pain points through formative research is critical in creating experiences that empower users to fulfill their goals.

I'm also a stickler for consistency and feel great UX comes from adhering to a well documented and maintained design system. Teach users your design language once, then stay out of their way. Good UX should be invisible.

I Believe in Knowing...

  • My users
  • Their needs and goals
  • Their daily minutiae
  • That I am not my user
  • Business goals

Because UX Research De-risks Projects

By having a deep understanding of the problem space, we minimize the risk and assumptions we bring into the solution space.
